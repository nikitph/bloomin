Governance, therefore, is the navigation of geodesics on a curved constitutional manifold.

We provide a rigorous mathematical derivation of the social metric, derive the Einstein-Social Field Equations, and present results from 20 comprehensive experiments that verify the existence of relativistic phenomena in social systems.
